# Agentic AI and the Financial Rails of the Future
**Justin Sun** (TRON) --- Monroe Stage

Hello everyone, thanks for being here. The talk right now will be recorded, just so you know. Enjoy. Hi, I'm Justin Sun, founder of Tron. Today I want to talk about how we can bridge crypto and the traditional finance to create a unified financial system. When I first got into crypto in 2012, blockchain was mostly an experiment among developers and researchers. But today, millions of people around the world rely on blockchain networks every day for payment, savings, trading, and digital commerce. The next stage of blockchain will be building the rails for unified financial system, where digital assets, traditional finance, and emerging technologies can operate on a shared infrastructure. Just as the internet create a common layer for global communication, blockchain is beginning to create a common layer for how value moves around the world. And one of the biggest drivers for this shift is AI. As AI become more common in everyday technology, the amount of machine-driven economy activity will grow quickly. AI systems are begin to perform real tasks in both business and consumer environments. Over time, autonomous AI agents will make decisions, execute transactions, and interact with global services for people. To support these activities, we need infrastructures that is fast, reliable, and global accessible. This is where blockchain networks like Tron can play an important role. The scale and efficiency of Tron network make it well-suited to support the level of the activities we expect from AI systems and AI agents. In many ways, Tron is the best solution for agentic AI payment infrastructure. This development also aligns with a larger goal, bring crypto and traditional finance. In markets where financial infrastructure is already strong and well-established, blockchain and AI can expand the system into a more open and programmable digital environment. New infrastructure has already been launched in Tron ecosystem. My example is BankofAI.io. This is an AI agent framework designed to help developers build and deploy autonomous AI agent on Tron network. The system use a layered and a scale design with a clear responsibility at each level. BankofAI already has more than one half a million users. It shows strong demand for tools that connect AI with blockchain infrastructure. Alongside this, we are also building a financial infrastructure layer that allow AI agent to execute on-chain payments, identity verification, and DeFi infrastructure on Tron and BNB network. Together, these systems show how blockchain can support new technologies like AI while helping digital assets network in the traditional financial system that built over many decades. Another important component of this unified financial system is stablecoin. Stablecoin are becoming the bridge between traditional financial system and the blockchain network. And across the Tron ecosystem, we are seeing strong momentum drive these trends. The Tron network has becoming a leading blockchain for stablecoin settlement and everyday digital payments. This is more than $85 billion in USDT on Tron with over 360 million user accounts on the blockchain. The network settles more than $22 billion in daily transaction volume. We are also seeing increasing participants for traditional financial institutions. For example, the PayPal USD stablecoin are launching on Tron network. And the Tron recently joined MasterCard Crypto Partner Program and collaboration on scaling innovation and connecting blockchain technology with practical application for people around the world. Of course, building financial rails require collaboration across the entire system. That's why integration remains a central focus within Tron ecosystem. Key integration including Metamask, Base, Cash, Chainlink, Near, Kraken, Wallet Connect, Ledger, WBTC, and many more. All of the integration on Tron strengthen the infrastructure that supports a unified infrastructure. As infrastructure grow, trust and security become equally important. A unified financial system must be safe and a responsible one. In September 2024, Tron, Tether, and TRM Labs launched a T3 financial crime unit, or T3FCU. And in a year, T3FCU analyzed 6 million of transactions globally, monitoring more than $3 billion US dollars in total volume and support freeze of over $300 million USD in illicit assets across five continents. This milestone shows blockchain technology has power to protect as much as it empower. Finally, building the rails for a unified financial system requires investing in a next generation of builders. Technology evolves quickly, but this is the people behind it who shape this future. TronDao has expanded collaboration with blockchain clubs at Columbia, Harvard, and Davos, building on an early partnership with MIT, Princeton, Yale, and UC Berkeley. Through this collaboration, we aiming to give students the tools, mentorship, and opportunities needs to build next generation of decentralized application. As we look ahead, the most important challenges is building the infrastructure that allows all parts of the financial system to work together. A unified financial system will combine the strengths of traditional finance with the openness and efficiency of blockchain network. It will support global payments, digital commerce, decentralized finance, and new economy activities driven by technology like AI. And this transformation will be built not by a single company or network. It will be built through collaboration. Thank you.

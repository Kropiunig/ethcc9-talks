# EthCC[9] Conference Transcripts

This repo contains transcripts of all talks from **EthCC[9]** (Ethereum Community Conference), held March 30 -- April 2, 2026 at the Palais des Festivals in Cannes, France.

## What's Here

- **~300 talks** transcribed from 7 livestream stages
- Each transcript is a markdown file in `transcripts/{stage}/`
- Files have YAML frontmatter: title, speaker, organization, stage, youtube URL
- `INDEX.md` has the full talk list organized by stage

## How to Search

- **By topic:** Grep across `transcripts/` for keywords (e.g. "MEV", "rollup", "stablecoin")
- **By speaker:** Grep for speaker names, or check frontmatter fields
- **By stage:** Browse `transcripts/{stage}/` directories
- **Overview first:** Read `INDEX.md` for the complete list of all talks with links

## Conference Structure

### Stages
Seven concurrent stages, named after film legends (venue was Palais des Festivals):
- **Chaplin** -- Main stage (DeFi Day panels, research talks)
- **Monroe** -- Largest track stage (DeFi, L2s, privacy, keynotes including Vitalik Buterin)
- **Redford** -- Stablecoins, MEV/PBS, institutional DeFi, rollups
- **Kelly** -- Euro stablecoins, DAOs, core protocol, post-quantum
- **Hepburn** -- Regulation, privacy, staking, PeerDAS, block building
- **Burton** -- RWA tokenization, intent-based trading, HFT, AI agents, ZK
- **Taylor** -- Real estate, DeFi security, formal verification, privacy protocols

### Schedule by Day
- **Monday March 30:** RWA Tokenisation, Stablecoins & Global Payments, DeFi, Regulation & Compliance
- **Tuesday March 31:** Built on Ethereum, Product & Marketers, Cypherpunk & Privacy, L2s, TERSE Symposium
- **Wednesday April 1:** Applied Cryptography, AI Agents & Automation, Core Protocol, Wild Cards
- **Thursday April 2:** Research, Security, ZK Tech & TEE

## Key Themes

When answering questions about "what happened at EthCC[9]", these were the dominant themes:
1. **Institutional arrival** -- Bloomberg, S&P, BNP Paribas, Euroclear, J.P. Morgan speakers for the first time
2. **L2 fragmentation** -- EEZ (Ethereum Economic Zone) vs Superchain as competing composability solutions
3. **Aave V4** -- Hub-and-spoke lending architecture launched on mainnet, $24B+ TVL
4. **Vitalik's "Real Decentralization"** -- Walk-away test, insider attack test, trusted computing base
5. **MiCA implementation** -- EU regulation shaping institutional DeFi adoption
6. **AI agents in DeFi** -- Dedicated track on autonomous agents, x402 protocol, agent economy
7. **Post-quantum readiness** -- Multiple talks on PQ signatures, FHE, ZK
8. **Stablecoin competition** -- Euro stablecoins, non-USD store-of-value, regulatory frameworks
